Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

How does the AI matching work?

Our AI analyzes job seekers' profiles, including skills, roles, and achievements, and matches them with employers' requirements. It uses advanced algorithms to determine the best fit, providing a match percentage.

Can I customize the AI-generated questionnaire?

Yes, the AI-generated questionnaire is tailored to the job seeker's profile, but employers can also add custom questions to better assess candidates.

How does the interview scheduling feature work?

Employers can set up interviews directly through the platform. The interview details will be sent to the matched job seekers, and both parties can manage their schedules through their dashboards.

What if a job seeker doesn't meet the match criteria?

Employers can set a minimum match percentage (e.g., 80%). Job seekers below this threshold will not be shown as qualified candidates, but they can still improve their profiles using our friends app

Is my data secure on SkillMatch AI?

Absolutely. We use advanced encryption and secure servers to protect all user data. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Can I see a list of potential employers before applying?

Yes, job seekers can view companies that align with their skills and roles, helping them target their job search more effectively.
